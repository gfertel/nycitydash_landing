NYCityDash

Turn your tablet into a beautiful subway countdown clock that you can rely on

NYCityDash is a free iPad and Android tablet app providing real time subway, CitiBike, and weather information. It is designed to be kept on 24/7 and placed in a prominent location in your home. Then, whenever you're leaving the apartment, you'll have your personalized transit information right in front of you.
